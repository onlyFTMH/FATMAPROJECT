BAKING

I really love baking and enjoy trying recipes from around the world, not just traditional ones. Cheesecake in particular — I consider myself an artist at it. If you ask me about my favorite dessert, I'd say cake with berry sauce and light cream is the best.

EDITING

Editing used to be my favorite hobby, especially video and audio editing. It helped me express feelings I couldn't share any other way. Nowadays, I don't edit as much, but I'm still interested in it and I follow a lot of edits on social media.

PLAYING PIANO

Playing the piano brings me so much peace. Even though I'm still learning, I enjoy exploring different melodies and practicing songs I love. It's one of the most relaxing and rewarding things I do. It feels like I'm pouring all my emotions into the song when I play my favorite pieces. It's such a joyful experience!

PROGRAMMING

Programming might be the most challenging hobby for me, but it's truly enjoyable. Especially in university, I feel a sense of competition with my classmates, and my grades definitely show my love for coding. It can be exhausting sometimes, but when I see the results of my hard work and study turn into something real, I feel a great sense of achievement.
